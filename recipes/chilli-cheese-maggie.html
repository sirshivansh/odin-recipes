Description

Chilli Cheese Maggie is a spicy and cheesy twist on the classic instant noodles. This dish combines the convenience of Maggi noodles with the bold flavors of green chilies and the creamy richness of melted cheese. It's a quick and delicious snack or meal that's perfect for those who love a little heat and indulgence in their food. The combination of spicy chilies and gooey cheese creates a delightful contrast that makes this dish irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or processed cheese)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a pan. Once the water starts boiling, add the Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the Maggi tastemaker (spice mix) that comes with the packet. Stir well.
  3. Add the finely chopped green chilies to the noodles. Adjust the quantity based on your spice preference.
  4. Add butter to the noodles and mix well to enhance the flavor.
  5. Sprinkle black pepper powder and salt to taste. Mix everything thoroughly.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and add the grated cheese on top of the noodles. Cover the pan with a lid and let it sit for 2-3 minutes until the cheese melts.
  7. Once the cheese has melted, give the noodles a good stir to combine all the flavors.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your spicy and cheesy Maggi noodles!
